WHAT IS THERMIRF™?

ThermiRF™ is a temperature controlled radio-frequency device used to tighten, smooth, and sculpt the skin. Radio-frequency is type of electrical energy that can be specifically controlled and thereby delivered very accurately to stimulate collagen production in the targeted area. Radio frequency energy has been used in medicine extensively, including treatments of the heart and nerves in the back for pain management. At FACES+, we pride ourselves in bringing patients the latest and most innovative cosmetic treatments. Our team of renowned dermatologists now offer patients a new minimally-invasive aesthetic treatment! Patients with sagging skin, wrinkles, and excess fat deposits can now benefit from the revolutionary ThermiLift™ in San Diego.

WHAT IS THERMIRF™ USED FOR?

There are 3 different treatments that can be delivered by ThermiRF™:

  1. Thermi Smooth™ is used to stimulate collagen production to soften lines and wrinkles on the face. The Thermi Smooth™ handheld probe delivers energy in a temperature controlled fashion and works to soften fine lines and wrinkles around the eyes, mouth or other facial areas. It promotes three different effects: 1) Immediate contraction of collagen, 2) Collagen remodeling and stretching, 3) Long-term stimulation resulting in new collagen production. The procedure takes 6-8 minutes per treatment area (per eye or upper lip) to perform. It is pain-free, requires no downtime, and patients leave without any redness or bruising. The treatments are gentle, relaxing and feel like a warm massage. A series of 3 - 6 treatments are performed a week or so apart to achieve optimal cosmetic results.
  2. Thermi Tight™ is used to remove excess fat and tighten loose skin on the neck or body. Thermi Tight™ is a radio-frequency device that is inserted under the skin that works to tighten the connective tissue. During the treatment, external and internal temperatures are monitored to ensure accuracy, precision, and safety. Thermi Tight™ works by heating up the tissue to the optimal temperature which stimulates new collagen production. Most procedures last around one hour. Thermi Tight™ can be used to treat almost any area of stubborn fat, particularly those that don’t respond to diet and exercise, such as laxity of the neck and jowls, loose skin on the abdomen, tops of knees, thighs, back fat, and under the arms. It has the dual capability of both fat removal and skin tightening offering an ideal treatment for body contouring and skin tightening without requiring additional treatment modalities. Results are typically seen within two to four months. Results may vary.This procedure can even be used for axillary hyperhidrosis (increased sweating under the arms).
  3. Thermi Rase™ is used to relax the frown area giving a long term Botox-like effect without any toxins! Thermi Rase™ is a two part procedure. The physician first maps the nerves using an external stimulator to see the exact location of the nerve pulses. This allows for precise lesions to be created during the procedure. The physician will inject a local anesthetic, much like a dentist uses, to numb the areas. Then, the physician inserts the radio-frequency device under the skin to target the nerve. This process is repeated several times until the patient is no longer able to make a frown. The entire procedure takes around one hour and clinical studies have shown the effects of the procedure may last 12 months or more. Results may vary.

PRP THERAPY (PLATELET RICH PLASMA)

PRP, or platelet rich plasma, is a substance derived from your own blood that contains high levels of platelets, the cells in our blood that assist in blood clotting, contain growth factors, and assist in cell regeneration. PRP has been used for many years in orthopedic medicine and dentistry due to its ability to accelerate healing. More recently, PRP has become popular in aesthetic medicine due to its ability to stimulate collagen development and cell regeneration. PRP can help with a wide array of cosmetic concerns including improvement in skin texture, scarring, thickness, and hair loss.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Prior to treatment, a blood sample will be taken from you. The blood is then placed into a centrifuge which spins the blood and separates the PRP from the rest of the blood. The PRP can then be applied to the skin topically in conjunction with microneedling or laser, or injected into the skin where the platelets create a mesh that stimulates wound healing and collagen production over time.

ADDITONAL PROCEDURES

PRP is almost always combined with other treatments such as microneedling, laser, fillers, and fat transfer to provide significant improvements in skin texture and thickness, scarring, restoration of facial volume and hair loss. Please schedule a consultation today to find out if PRP therapy is right for you.

RECOVERY TIME

Recovery time depends on the type of PRP treatment that was performed. The downtime for topical PRP and microneedling is usually 1-3 days, topical PRP and laser is usually 3-7 days. Patients receiving injectable PRP treatments may experience mild bruising and swelling with downtime between 3-5 days. Results are visible after several weeks.

HOW MANY TREATMENTS ARE REQUIRED?

For skin rejuvenation, optimal results are seen after 2-3 treatments spaced 3-4 weeks apart, followed by an annual treatment for maintenance thereafter. Patients with more extensive collagen loss will typically need a series of 3-4 treatments every 3-4 weeks, followed by maintenance treatments twice a year. For hair loss, 3-6 treatments spaced 4 weeks are needed. More difficult cases may need additional treatments.

MICRONEEDLING

Microneedling, also called collagen induction therapy, is a method of skin rejuvenation using a motorized device with multiple fine needles to create uniform punctures in the skin surface. The tiny punctures created by the needles extend down 0.5–3 mm into the skin and stimulate the body to produce new collagen and elastin over time. The result is an improvement in skin texture and firmness, reduction in pore size, and softening of acne scars and stretch marks.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Your face will be cleansed to remove any makeup and impurities. An anesthetic cream will be applied to the entire treatment area for 30-45 minutes to make the procedure more comfortable. After removal of the anesthetic cream, a gel containing skin-stimulating peptides will be applied to the treatment area. The microneedling device is then moved across the skin surface in multiple directions for 10-15 minutes, creating thousands of microscopic channels into the skin and allowing for deeper penetration of the peptide gel. Slight bleeding may occur, but this is normal and desirable as it indicates stimulation of blood vessels that will induce new collagen formation. Following the procedure, a soothing serum will be applied to moisturize and protect the skin.

ADDITONAL PROCEDURES

Microneedling can be combined with PRP, neurotoxins, fillers, fat transfer and skin tightening procedures for even better results based on your specific needs. Please schedule a consultation today to find out if microneedling is right for you.

RECOVERY TIME

Your face will be red following your microneedling treatment, similar to a sunburn. Slight bruising or swelling may occur but is less common. For most people, these symptoms will resolve within 48-72 hours. Mineral-based makeup and sunscreen SPF 30+ can be applied the day after microneedling, but products containing retinol or acids (glycolic, salicylic, etc) should be avoided for at least 3 days post-treatment.

HOW MANY TREATMENTS ARE REQUIRED?

Since the results from microneedling are cumulative, multiple treatments are strongly encouraged to achieve the best outcome. For skin rejuvenation, an initial series of four treatments spaced 3-4 weeks apart is recommended, followed by a treatment every 3 months for maintenance. For more difficult conditions to treat such as acne scarring or stretch marks, multiple series of treatments may be required.

RADIESSE®

Radiesse® (Radiance) is a pure, synthetic compound with a record of safety that extends more than 25 years. It is comprised of microspheres of calcium hydroxylapatite, combined with an aqueous gel. The key element of Radiesse® is identical to the chemical structure of calcium naturally present in the human body. Throughout its life, Radiesse® remains soft and flexible and also exceptionally compatible with the body.
Unlike temporary treatments that must be repeated, Radiesse® may provide years of rejuvenation. However, the resorption rate is variable and we have had some patients in whom the material was gone by 6 months. It is gradually replaced by the body’s own tissue, and slowly dissolves away in some cases up to seven years later. Radiesse® is FDA-approved for soft tissue marking in any anatomical location, vocal cord injection and oral and maxillofacial augmentation. FDA approval for facial implant is pending. Internationally, Radiesse has received approval for facial implant and treatment of stress urinary incontinence.

JUVEDERM®

As we age, our skin changes. Over time, the natural volume of youthful skin begins to diminish as wrinkles and folds form. But, with Juvederm® injectable gel, you don’t have to just sit back and let it happen! Approved by the FDA, Juvederm® injectable gel is a “next-generation” dermal filler. The smooth consistency gel helps to instantly restore your skin’s volume and smooth away facial wrinkles and folds, like your “smile lines” or “parentheses” (nasolabial folds – the creases that run from the bottom of your nose to the corners of your mouth).
Juvederm® is a smooth consistency gel made of hyaluronic acid – a naturally occurring substance in your skin that helps to add volume and hydration. Your healthcare professional eases Juvederm® injectable gel under the skin to provide a smooth, natural look and feel – so everyone will notice (but no one will know)! In fact, studies show superior results of Juvederm® compared to a popular collagen-based dermal filler. And, almost 90% of all study volunteers who were treated with both Juvederm® injectable gel and another popular dermal filler preferred Juvederm®! In addition, Juvederm® injectable gel is the first FDA-approved hyaluronic acid dermal filler that is proven to be safe and effective for persons of color.

RESTYLANE®

Restylane® is a crystal-clear, non-animal, biodegradable gel based on a natural substance, called hyaluronic acid. The gel is injected into the skin in tiny amounts with a very fine needle. The result is instantaneous and produces a long-lasting, natural enhancement that is gentle and safe to your skin.
Restylane will last from 6 to 12 months and is very effective for diminishing wrinkles around the corners of the mouth, the lips, the nasolabial folds and the frown lines. Restylane is also an excellent temporary filler for lip augmentation. Experience shows that it is effective for up to six months when injected in the lips. Restylane is a long-lasting, but not permanent, result, leaving you the option of adjusting your treatment in tune with changing taste and fashion.
